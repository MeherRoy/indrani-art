Deliverance

23 April, 2020 • 1 Min Read
Seasons around the globe Encapsulate the earth by their ebb and flow. Your Divine presence I feel When their astounding beauty I see.
Spring is the King of them all Colourful seasons enthral Nature's priceless gift to mankind To enliven him it is designed.
Season of Spring this year Visited us in all its grandeur But nature was so much provoked It stopped us from getting soaked.
Calendar year Twenty-Twenty Played havoc, was not at all trendy Kept us confined in our house Left us alone with a grouse.
An invincible enemy unknown, unseen Through it's malfeasance took away the sheen. Left us with changed priorities Focusing only on health anxieties.
We all missed moving around To see the wonders of spring abound. A season of visual drought Which so long was unheard about.
Time may have come to look inward Realise that we have gone wayward Perhaps we need to cleanse our souls And very quickly re-set our goals.
Nothing happens without Your Command We all rightly understand. If this is a reflection of Your annoyance, We must sit in prayer and seek Deliverance.
