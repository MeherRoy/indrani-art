Grandchild

24 October, 2020 • 1 Min Read
Red Adenium flower
See this little flower, Strikingly pink in colour. Blossomed in my garden, Start of Navratri Celebrations.
It was a bright morning, Sun was just rising. Was awe-struck by a momentary feeling Of Her Divine presence embracing.
Sensing this to be a sign of hope, I promptly took my i-phone. And there lay in store, News of birth the day before.
On the birth of a grand daughter we rejoice, Said my friend in a choked voice. With excitement my eyes felt moist, Mother Goddess had indicated Her choice.
I have become a Nani, Said my friend in ecstasy. "Her face is such a roly poly, Oh! My God, She looks just like me".
Cheers to you my friend, You'll see more of her at day's end. Enjoy her blooming is what I recommend, She'll be all yours till she gets a boyfriend.
