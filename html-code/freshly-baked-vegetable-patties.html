Freshly Baked Vegetable Patties

29 June, 2020 • 1 Min Read
Freshly baked vegetable patties In lieu of dal and hot chapattis, Coupled with chilled fruit punch, Made a sumptuous Sunday lunch.
Our excitement knew no limits When this menu got fixed. We drove to the market To buy all provisions in a basket.
Potatoes were boiled and mashed; Peas were peeled and bashed, Both got cooked together With salt, spices and coriander.
This mix smelt so tempting Was surely an appropriate stuffing For the dough of refined flour Prepared with margarine, salt and water.
We rolled the dough in rectangles And spread the filling from all angles One side got folded over the other And put for baking in hot temperature.
The aroma of baking Left our mouths watering. Hot and crispy vegetable patties Made the family happy and chatty.
An overjoyed family came running To the dining table and stood surrounding. Clattering of dishes and clinking of glasses Effectively mesmerised all our senses.
